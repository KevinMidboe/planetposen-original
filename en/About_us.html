About us

 
 



We make a fabric gift bag that is durable enough to be reused time after time, year after year.


At Planetposen, we believe it’s possible to take care of the planet without being boring & we’ve chosen fabrics & ribbons in fun patterns & colors.  We’re sure you’ll find a gift bag for any occasion or person.


 




 

We are a tiny cottage company that cares about the environment & wants to do our part to reduce waste.
